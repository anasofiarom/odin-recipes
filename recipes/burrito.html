Chofi's Melting Burrito

This is how it will probably look like

Description

This recipe by my muchacha a long time ago

Really practical for cooking in the morning when you're in a hurry and hungry

Ingredients

  • Tortilla
  • Frijoles fritos
  • Salsa
  • Cebolla

Steps

  1. Heat tortilla and frijoles
  2. Put frijoles in the tortilla
  3. Add salsa and cebolla as you please
