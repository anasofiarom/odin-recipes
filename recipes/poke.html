Description

This recipe was given to me by my grandmother Win Lu of the great China, she loves rice and tuna and wasabi

I always cook it for meals with a lot of people when its hard to share

Ingredients

  • Tuna
  • Wasabi
  • Rice
  • Love

Steps

  1. Cook rice
  2. Unfreeze tuna
  3. Mix and add Wasabi
